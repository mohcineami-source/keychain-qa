# 01 — Brand Positioning

## Brand

- **Name (AR):** ميدالية رقم السيارة
- **Product:** custom acrylic keychain shaped/printed with the customer's Qatar car plate.
- **Header text:** the site uses brand text "ميدالية رقم السيارة" (no heavy logo at launch).

## Positioning statement

A premium, personal keepsake of something every Qatari driver is proud of — their plate. Simple to order, fast to receive, fairly priced with delivery included. The brand sits at the intersection of **Qatari heritage** and **Apple-style simplicity**: warm and local in feel, minimal and effortless in execution.

## Who it's for

Qatar-based car owners who value personalization and a clean buying experience: a quick mobile-first flow, no account creation, no clutter, WhatsApp-based human follow-up.

## Brand pillars

1. **Personal** — it's *your* plate, *your* car: "خل رقم موترك دوم معاك."
2. **Premium but honest** — elegant acrylic product, premium UI, no fake luxury or hype.
3. **Fast & local** — produced and delivered inside Qatar within 24–48 hours, delivery included.
4. **Effortless** — one focused funnel, minimal fields, WhatsApp handoff for the rest.

## Tone of voice

- Clean Gulf Arabic with **Qatari flavor** (e.g., "موترك", "نوصلها لين عندك").
- Premium, direct, human. Short sentences.
- Never cheesy, never overhyped, never fake-scarce.

**On-brand phrases:**
- خل رقم موترك دوم معاك
- تفصيل خاص لرقم لوحتك
- جاهزة وتوصلك خلال 24–48 ساعة
- السعر شامل التوصيل داخل قطر

**Off-brand / banned phrases:**
- الأفضل في العالم
- عرض لا يتكرر
- آخر فرصة
- باقي 3 فقط
- رسمي ومعتمد

## Visual identity (high level)

- **Qatari maroon** (`#8A1538`) as the signature accent over a light premium base (soft warm gray `#F7F5F3`, white cards).
- Generous spacing, rounded corners, subtle borders, minimal motion.
- Arabic-first typography (one clean font), RTL throughout.
- Product imagery is premium and clean; placeholders are used until real photography exists. No fake government/official plate marks.

Full system: `docs/04-design-system.md`.

## Trust & honesty rules

The brand earns trust through clarity and speed, **not** through fabricated proof. Do not claim official plate licensing, government affiliation, certifications, guaranteed delivery beyond what's controllable, or display invented reviews/testimonials. If/when real proof exists, it can be added — until then, lead with the offer, the price transparency, and the delivery promise.
